WHO ARE WE

Smart sense Youth Development is a non profit organization based in Soshanguve north of Tshwane. We are a youth-led organization that seeks to create a platform for the youth of South Africa to participate in exploring young peoples economic political and community life, responding to the growing society needs in a positive manner.

"Our mission is to develop youth to become ethical leaders."

"Our vision is to develop literate, creative citizens that will build better communities."
